What is the Seal?

Crafted from bronze and with a diameter of 6.5 feet, the Davis Centennial Seal serves as a tribute to the City of Davis on its 100th anniversary. It honors the history of this land and its people throughout the years.

What does it mean?

The 10 radiating panels delve into themes and values that bind us to our past. These include aspiration, community, cooperation, compassion, leadership, innovation, engagement, global citizenship, stewardship, vision, and optimism.
